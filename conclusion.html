CONCLUSION


Everything Everywhere All at Once is a story with many layers. It has become a breakout hit because the film resonates with people in different ways whether it's because of the characters or the themes.

Aside from the structure, other non-typical-Hollywood factors made the film's success surprising. It had a low budget and a unique style while exploring Asian American identity. It's no surprise that the film became the most awarded of all time and made Oscars history.

There are endless ways to dissect Everything Everywhere All at Once. Maybe with multiple viewings, you'll discover something new every time.
