Everything Everywhere All at Once is a cinematic experience that can leave viewers both emotional and confused. It can be hard to follow the main plot in just one viewing. What do bagels and googly eyes mean? Why do we cry at rocks talking to each other? This project is for fans (like myself) who want a guide that make sense of the filmâ€™s chaos.

Every line of the film has been transcribed, timestamped, and analyzed. Breaking from traditionally written film analysis, this project takes a visual data-driven approach focusing on the characters, the themes, the structure, and the universes. Ultimately, I hope it gives fans a deeper appreciation for the film and its complexity!
